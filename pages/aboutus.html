About Crazy Hat Studios


Founded by Russ Messer, Crazy Hat Studios is a film studio focused on high-quality storytelling through camera work, production, directing, and writing. Known for unique visuals and compelling narratives, the studio brings stories to life with cinematic flair.

Our Mission


To captivate audiences with innovative, memorable films that set a new standard in independent filmmaking.

About the Founder


Russ Messer is passionate about his craft with years of experience in lighting, writing, directing, and filming his personal projects. Russ is based in O'Fallon, Illinois and enjoys multiple hobbies including football, longboarding, basketball, and spending time with friends and family.
