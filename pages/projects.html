Opening Ceremony Live Viewing & Gala Night

Join us for an exclusive live viewing of the Olympic Opening Ceremony, followed by a glamorous gala night. Experience the magic of the opening with a grand LED screen display and live commentary from seasoned sports analysts. Post-ceremony, enjoy a luxurious gala with thematic entertainment, gourmet cuisine, and an opportunity to mingle with former Olympic athletes and sports personalities.

Olympic Legends Talk Series

Omicron Media Unlimited presents a series of inspiring talks featuring Olympic legends. Listen to stories of triumph, resilience, and the spirit of the Olympics directly from the athletes who lived them. Each session includes a keynote speech, a Q&A segment, and a chance to meet and greet these inspiring individuals.

Behind-the-Scenes Media Tour

Get an exclusive behind-the-scenes look at how Olympic media coverage is brought to life. This guided tour takes you through the live broadcasting studios, the production rooms, and the heart of the media center. Gain insights into the cutting-edge technology and creative processes behind broadcasting the world’s biggest sporting event.

Virtual Reality Olympic Expereinces

Step into the world of the Olympics like never before with our Virtual Reality experience. Immerse yourself in simulated Olympic events, get a 360-degree view of various Olympic venues, and enjoy interactive games that make you feel like an Olympic athlete.

Olympic Photography Exhibition

Explore a captivating exhibition showcasing iconic moments from Olympic history, captured by renowned photographers. This event features a curated collection of powerful images that tell the story of the Olympic Games’ most memorable moments, triumphs, and emotions.

Olympic Film Festival

Celebrate the spirit of the Olympics with a film festival dedicated to movies and documentaries about the Games. From historical documentaries to inspiring stories of athletes and the global impact of the Olympics, this film festival offers a diverse range of perspectives on the world of sports.
